I am a PhD student in the the NICTA Optimisation Research Laboratory at Monash University, Melbourne. For my project, I investigate the mechanism and optimality of infrastructure construction in ants, using a combination of field and laboratory experiments and mathematical modeling techniques.

When not working on self-organisation processes in ants, I enjoy meddling with swarm robot control and social network analysis.
